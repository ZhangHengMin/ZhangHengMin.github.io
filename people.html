    Hengmin Zhang 张恒敏

    Zhang Hengmin, PhD, Professor, PhD Supervisor, and a recipient of the National High-Level Young Talents Program. He obtained his bachelor’s and master’s degrees in Mathematics, and later earned his Doctor of Engineering degree in Control Science and Engineering from the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanjing University of Science and Technology. After graduating with his PhD, he first conducted postdoctoral research at the School of Information Science and Engineering, East China University of Science and Technology, and then pursued further postdoctoral research at the Faculty of Science and Technology, University of Macau, and the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Nanyang Technological University.

Positions

We look forward to hearing from passionate and driven candidates.

PhD / MS in Control Science and Engineering

Admission Expectations (招生期望)：Potential candidates are expected to possess a solid foundation in mathematics, strong programming capabilities, and proficiency in English. Prior research experience, including participation in academic competitions, publication of scholarly work, or engagement in extracurricular research activities will be regarded as a significant advantage.

Training Objectives (培养目标)：Through systematic and rigorous research training, students will develop rigorous scientific thinking, strong practical competencies, and a broad academic perspective. They will cultivate the ability to independently identify, analyze, and address research problems. Students are also expected to produce high-impact scholarly work in leading journals and conferences, and are encouraged to pursue continued engagement in academic research.
